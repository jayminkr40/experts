About Bailiff Advice UK

Learn about our mission, values, and how we provide free bailiff advice

Our Mission

Bailiff Advice UK was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide free, reliable, and accessible advice to people in England and Wales who are dealing with bailiffs or enforcement agents.

We understand how stressful and intimidating it can be when bailiffs are involved, and many people don't know their rights or where to turn for help. Our service was created to bridge this gap.

What We Do

Our team of trained advisors provides free information about:

  • Your legal rights when dealing with bailiffs
  • What bailiffs can and cannot do
  • How to challenge unfair bailiff actions or fees
  • Negotiating affordable payment plans
  • Stopping bailiffs from entering your property
  • Understanding different types of bailiff warrants

Important Information

Please note: Bailiff Advice UK provides information and guidance based on current legislation in England and Wales. We are not a law firm and cannot provide legal representation. For complex legal matters, we recommend consulting a solicitor specializing in debt or consumer law.
