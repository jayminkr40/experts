Urgent Bailiff Situations

If bailiffs are at your door right now or you have received notice that bailiffs will be visiting imminently:

  • Call our helline immediately: 0800 123 4567 (press 1 for urgent situations)
  • If calling outside business hours, leave a detailed voicemail and we'll call you back as soon as possible
  • Check our homepage for emergency tips while waiting for a callback

What to Include When Contacting Us

To help us provide the best advice, please include:

  • The type of debt (council tax, parking fines, CCJ, etc.)
  • Whether bailiffs have already visited or if you've just received notice
  • Any important dates (when you received notices, when bailiffs might visit)
  • Your general location (town/city) in England or Wales

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during weekdays. Urgent situations (bailiffs at the door) are prioritized and we respond as quickly as possible.

Is your advice really free?

Yes, all advice from Bailiff Advice UK is completely free. We never charge for our services or information.

Can you represent me in court?

No, we provide information and guidance only. We are not solicitors and cannot provide legal representation. For court matters, we recommend consulting a qualified solicitor.

Do you cover Scotland or Northern Ireland?

Our advice is specific to England and Wales. Bailiff laws differ in Scotland (where they are called "sheriff officers") and Northern Ireland.
